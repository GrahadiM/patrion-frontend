Cerita (Story)

In an age where myths breathe and legends walk among us, The First Guardian chronicles the perilous journey of Satria. Born into a world teetering on the brink of annihilation by the Void Legion, Satria discovers an ancient artifact—the Sun Shield—that chooses him as its bearer.

Reluctant at first, he must master the forgotten arts of the light to unite the fractured tribes of the Nusantara archipelago. But power comes at a cost. As the darkness grows stronger, Satria faces a choice that will echo through eternity: sacrifice his humanity to save the world, or remain human and watch it burn.

This cinematic masterpiece lays the foundation for the entire PATRION universe, introducing key characters like The Sage of Bromo and the Shadow Walker, who will play pivotal roles in future installments.
