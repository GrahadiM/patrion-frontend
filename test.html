Story Synopsis

Prologue: The Shattering

"Following the Great Shattering, the realms of Aethelgard have fallen into a fragile peace. Yet, in the shadows of the royal courts, a new darkness stirs..."

You play as a young diplomat thrust into the center of a conspiracy that threatens to tear the world apart once more. Your weapon is not a sword, but your words and your heart.

Navigate complex social webs, uncover hidden truths, and forge unbreakable bonds with a cast of diverse characters. Will you pursue the path of duty, or follow the call of forbidden love? In Aethelgard Chronicles, every conversation is a battlefield, and every choice leaves a scar.
